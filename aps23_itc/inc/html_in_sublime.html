Information Technology Investment Procedure

All University of Washington (UW) technology projects and acquisitions are goverened by the University of Washingtons Policy on Information Technology, Telecommunications and Networking Projects and Acquisistions (abbreviated APS 2.3). Certain processes and approvals may by required, depending on the type and size of the technology expenditures you need to make.

These webpages help you determine what approvals and other processes are required under APS 2.3.

Before you begin: Contact your internal department administrator to determine how the department handles new IT projects.
